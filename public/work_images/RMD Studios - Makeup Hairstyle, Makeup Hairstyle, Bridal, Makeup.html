About Us

  Hi, I’m Ria Minhas, a professional freelance hair and make-up artist, specializing in a wide range of  events and styles from relaxed romantic looks to glamorous bridal hair and makeup.


I’m available for various on-site make-up and hair artistry needs, including glam bridal and semi-bridal events, special occasions and photo shoots.


Based out of Vancouver B.C, my studio is located in west Abbotsford. I’m also more than happy to travel where needed. Serving clients from across locations in B.C including Langley, Surrey , Abbotsford and Vancouver.
